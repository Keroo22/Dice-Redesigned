About Dice Group

A vertically integrated manufacturing powerhouse

Dice Group stands as one of the region's most prominent industrial manufacturing groups, with a diverse portfolio spanning apparel production, textile manufacturing, retail operations, and sustainable practices. Dice Group has been ranked as the 5th top manufacturer and exporter for knitted garments in Egypt with +110M (USD) annual turnover in 2025.

Our vertically integrated approach enables us to control every aspect of the production chain, from raw materials to finished products, ensuring the highest quality standards and operational efficiency. This enables the company to hold a strong chain of well-branded buyers in US and EU markets, which are in strategic business partnership with Dice Group.

With decades of experience and a commitment to innovation, we continue to drive growth and create value for our stakeholders, employees, and communities.

Corporate Evolution

From company establishment to vertical integration and retail expansion

Company Establishment (1989) Vertical Integration Phase (1992 - 2002) Retail Arm (2011) Printing (2012) Integration & Expansion (2015 - 2017)

1989

Company Establishment

DICE Sport and Casual Wear was established by the Toma family.

1992 - 1994

DICE 1 & DICE 2

Established the first two production factories.

1998

Knitting Facility

Added knitting capacity with a production level of 70 tons per month.

2002

Dyeing Facility

Established the Master Line dyeing facility.

2006

DICE 3

Added the third production facility, DICE 3.

2008

National Textile

National Textile, majority owned by Grandview, acquired a majority stake in the company.

2011

Retail Arm

Introduced DICE retail to the Egyptian retail market and added the fourth production facility, DICE 4.

2012

Screen Printing

Established the screen print house.

2013

DICE 5 & Certification

Established DICE 5 and received RINA certification.

2015

Value Proposition

Completed a series of acquisitions to complement the value proposition.

2016

Integration Phase

Successfully integrated acquired entities into the value chain.

2017

Expansion

Acquired Network for Ready-Made Garments, which became the DICE Kafr El Dawar factory.

2023

Misr Helwan Lease

Leased the Misr Helwan Textile Factory.

2025

Misr Helwan MOU

Signed a memorandum of understanding to acquire the Misr Helwan Textile Factory and received 60% of the land according to the MOU.
